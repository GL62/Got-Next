Got Next?

Got Next is an app that gives real time information about basketball courts near users.Specifically, Got Next will show users about the conditions of courts, players on court, and the quality of the players.

Got Next was founded by three teenagers from the DMV area who just loved to play basketball. Too many times people want to play basketball but do not have the information to play a full game. With Got Next, people will be able to play pick up basketball whenever they would like.
